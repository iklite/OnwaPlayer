🎵 Welcome to Onwa Media Player

Share your favorite music, videos, and photos with friends nearby — without using the internet. Onwa Media Player lets you connect directly to other devices using WiFi Direct (P2P), so you can share media and chat instantly.

✨ Get Started in 4 Easy Steps

📝 1. Register Your Profile

Before joining a group, register by providing a unique username and your real name. This information is only used to identify you within the P2P group and is never transmitted outside the group. It helps everyone recognize you during chats and media sharing.

📡 2. Enable WiFi P2P

Tap the WiFi P2P button in the app to turn on sharing mode. Your device will start searching for nearby devices.

🤝 3. Connect to a Friend

Select a nearby device from the list. Once connected, you can start sharing media files or chat instantly.

🎧 4. Share, Sync, and Chat

As a group owner, you can transmit whatever media you are viewing or listening to directly to all connected clients. If you are watching a video or listening to audio, it will automatically play on their devices too!

Clients can also download the currently playing media to their own device with just a tap of the download button in the network panel.

🌟 Enjoy seamless sharing, real-time media sync, and chatting without the need for mobile data or WiFi networks.

🎶 Happy Sharing! 🎵📸🎥
